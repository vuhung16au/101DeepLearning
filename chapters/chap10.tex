\chapter{Sequence Modeling: Recurrent and Recursive Nets}
\label{chap:sequence-modeling}

This chapter covers architectures designed for sequential and temporal data, including recurrent neural networks (RNNs) and their variants.


\begin{learningobjectives}
\objective{Why sequence models are needed and data modalities requiring temporal context}
\objective{Comparison of vanilla RNNs, LSTMs, and GRUs with their gating mechanisms}
\objective{Backpropagation through time (BPTT) and truncated BPTT with gradient clipping}
\objective{Sequence-to-sequence models with attention and alignment mechanisms}
\objective{Advanced decoding: bidirectional RNNs, teacher forcing, and beam search}
\objective{Common failure modes and mitigation strategies}
\end{learningobjectives}
