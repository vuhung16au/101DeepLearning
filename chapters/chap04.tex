\chapter{Numerical Computation}
\label{chap:numerical-computation}

This chapter covers numerical methods and computational considerations essential for implementing deep learning algorithms. Topics include gradient-based optimization, numerical stability, and conditioning.

\begin{learningobjectives}
\objective{Numerical precision issues and solutions for overflow, underflow, and numerical instability in deep learning}
\objective{Gradient-based optimization and the role of Jacobian and Hessian matrices in optimization landscapes}
\objective{Constrained optimization using Lagrange multipliers and KKT conditions for regularization}
\objective{Numerical stability assessment and gradient checking techniques}
\objective{Practical numerical techniques including log-sum-exp tricks and mixed precision training}
\objective{Common numerical problems in deep learning and their solutions}
\end{learningobjectives}
